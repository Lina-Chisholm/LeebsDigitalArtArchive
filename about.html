Past

Leebs started creating as a kindergartener, drawing and making art as kids do. However, they knew from a very young age that they wanted to be an artist and take the art in thier books into the real world. In highschool they started doing digital art and have been doing it ever since, with a focus on original character building. They started building community, learning from art classes, other students and online resources. In university they studied Animation and learned of the commecial world behind art and more artistic techniques. They continued to hone thier skills and build a portfolio, eventually starting to take commissions and share their work online.

Present

Leebs is currently working on digital art commissions and has a growing portfolio of work. They have leaned into the Vtubing community, trialing thier art with rigging and animation. With hopes of making others ideas and concepts come to life through their art. Leebs is also active on social media, sharing their work and connecting with other artists and art enthusiasts. They are passionate about creating art that resonates with others and are always looking for new opportunities to grow and improve as an artist.

Future

Leebs hopes to continue growing their portfolio and expanding their artistic skills. They are interested in exploring more advanced techniques in digital art, animation, and character design. Their goal is to create more diverse and engaging artwork that resonates with a wider audience. Leebs also hopes to collaborate with other artists and creators in the future, potentially working on larger projects or even venturing into the world of game design or animation. They are excited about the possibilities that the future holds for their artistic journey and are committed to continuously improving and evolving as an artist.
